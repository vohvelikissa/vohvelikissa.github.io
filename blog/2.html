Making of brainfuck++

Project description

I started this project that is making an extended version of the completely useless and esoteric language that is brainfuck. The original design of the brainfuck language has only the characters +-,. and the less than and the greater than character The greater than sign increments the data pointer by one and the exact opposite sign does the exact opposite. Plus increases the byte at the data pointer by one, minus does the exact opposite. Dot outputs the byte at the data pointer. Comma takes one byte of input to the data pointer. Opening bracket and closing bracket is basically the looping mechanism of the brainfuck lang. I plan to make this basic setup better by adding the bash gateway to it by adding two characters to it. ( and ). Making the full list of usable characters in the language greater and less than, plus and minus, comma and dot, brackets and ().

What I learned while making

First off I had to learn the basic characters of the brainfuck language and then I had to extend that learning by adding the two characters that I added to the basic definitions to the list.

The project is as far as I want to take it now for now at least. The main takeaway for me was some functions that are in the std libs. I had fun.
