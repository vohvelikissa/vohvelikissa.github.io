My first experiences at trying to get clothing

It has been quite a week already at trying to get clothing that is both really nice and that fits me. I'm currently built like an absolute unit meaning that I am plus sized. That in itself would not be an issue if it didn't mean that all of my clothing options are the stuff that my mom likes to wear. OK, to be real here there are some really nice clothes that I discovered today while exploring 2 of my local UFF stores that fit me and that I want to wear, but they are not the majority in my size.

What I think that I need to do now

  • Learn to modify clothes
  • Go to the same stores more often
  • Explore new stores
  • Lose atleast some weight to fit cuter clothes
